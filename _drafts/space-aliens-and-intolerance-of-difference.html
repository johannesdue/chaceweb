A year ago, space watchers spotted something in the vicinity of our sun they couldn’t quite identify. They called it Oumuamua, and the way it moved suggested it wasn’t just an ordinary comet or asteroid. Harvard researchers now raise the possibility it “may be a fully operational probe sent intentionally to Earth vicinity by an alien civilization.”
